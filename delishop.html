calamansi FAQ

What is calamansi?

Calamansi is a citrus fruit, a hybrid between kumquat and mandarin. It is common in Malay and Filipino cuisine.

What does it taste like?

Calamansi is tangey, tart, like a cross between an orange and lime. Like Yuzu, Calamansi also has a distinct scent. In Thai, it is known as มะปิด.

Does it have any health benefit?

Calamansi is packed with vitamin C.

What can I make with calamansi syrup?

The possibility is endless. To start, you can mix it with yogurt, soda, or even soju. We're prepping our lists of scenarios and recipes. It will available in September.
